AI-Powered Climate Control with Blockchain-based Transparency

What is AgriGuard?

AgriGuard uses AI and blockchain technology to manage agricultural produce storage, predict market prices, and ensure product traceability. By integrating live weather data, our platform optimizes storage conditions and predicts spoilage risk, ensuring that perishable goods maintain their value over time.

With real-time smart auction alerts, AgriGuard empowers farmers, wholesalers, and traders with critical information to make informed decisions, thereby enhancing the efficiency and transparency of agricultural markets.
